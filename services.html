Our Process

How We Work With You

A clear, collaborative process from your first enquiry through to final delivery and beyond.

1

Enquiry & Requirement Analysis

You share your application, drawings or samples. Our team analyses the requirement to determine the optimal technical approach.

2

Compound Selection & Design

We recommend the appropriate rubber compound and develop tooling precisely aligned to your specifications and performance targets.

3

Prototyping & Approval

Samples are produced and submitted for your evaluation. Refinements are incorporated before full production commences.

4

Production & Quality Testing

Full production with continuous in-process quality control and final analytical laboratory inspection on every batch.

5

Delivery & After-Sales Support

Your order is delivered on schedule with ongoing technical support and dedicated service for all future requirements.

Quality Assurance

Laboratory Capabilities

Our in-house analytical laboratory is equipped with sophisticated instruments to verify product quality across all critical parameters.

PHY

Physical Testing

Tensile strength, elongation at break, hardness, compression set

THM

Thermal Analysis

Heat ageing, low-temperature flexibility, thermal resistance

CHM

Chemical Resistance

Fluid immersion, oil swell, media compatibility testing

DIM

Dimensional Inspection

Precision gauging and critical feature verification to specification

VIS

Visual Quality

Surface defect inspection and profile conformity checks
