Our Process

How We Work With You

A clear, collaborative process from your first enquiry to final delivery.

1

Enquiry & Requirement Analysis

You share your application, drawings or samples. Our team analyses the requirement to determine the optimal approach for your need.

2

Compound Selection & Design

3

Prototyping & Approval

Samples are produced and shared for your evaluation, with refinements incorporated before full production commences.

Delivery & After-Sales Support

Your order is delivered on schedule with ongoing technical support and service for all future requirements.
