Our Origin

Born from the Rubber Capital of India

Auto Polymers has its origin in the state of Kerala, India — a paradise for scenic beauty, and being evergreen, accounting for more than 93% of natural rubber (Hevea Brasiliensis) produced in India. The growing demand for technically competent moulded rubber products, previously available only in the global market, became a source of motivation and inspiration to a team of professionals with a high degree of material know-how, to manufacture and offer them domestically.

Auto Polymers, founded in 1993, is one of the leading manufacturers of natural and synthetic moulded rubber products — O-rings, oil seals, gaskets, grommets, bush kits, diaphragms, fabric-reinforced pads and more — for numerous applications including automotive, medical, industrial and commercial. The core team comprises individuals with decades of exposure in the rubber industry.

We set high standards for our products and consistently meet those standards through systems and processes that are environment-friendly. With a highly efficient proactive team of qualified and technically sound professionals, we provide innovative, competitive and complete solutions as per customer requirements and applications.
