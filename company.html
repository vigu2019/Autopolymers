Company Profile

Our story, our values, and what has driven three decades of consistent growth in rubber manufacturing.

Our Origin

Founded in the Heart of Kerala

Auto Polymers was established in 1993 at the Athani Industrial Estate, Thrissur — a city well known as the cultural and commercial hub of Kerala. Kerala itself is the dominant rubber-producing state in India, accounting for over 90% of the country's natural rubber output, with the rubber cultivation heartland centred in the Kottayam and Malappuram districts.

Our location in Thrissur places us in proximity to this rich natural resource base, enabling us to source the finest natural rubber for our manufacturing operations while benefiting from the skilled technical workforce that Kerala is widely recognised for.

From the outset, the company's founding vision was clear: to deliver complete solutions as per customer requirements and applications — a purpose that has guided every product and every partnership since inception.

Our Vision: To deliver complete, end-to-end rubber component solutions that consistently meet customer requirements — through innovation, quality and integrity.
